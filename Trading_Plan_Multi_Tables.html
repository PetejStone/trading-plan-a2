Position Sizing - A Setups (2%) - Room in ADR
- Room from other key levels (YH,YL,WH,WL)
- Unless it is a candle break through a Key Level at or near a break of a VWAP
- A2 Painted at or very close to a VWAP or a Key Level
- Strong closed candles off or through the HoD/LoD VWAP
- Strong inclines or declines in the Daily and other VWAPs
- Break through multiple VWAPs that are close together
- Strong VWAP break w/ inclining/falling VWAPs
- Multiple Barriers to Exit (another VWAP, Key Level)
Position Sizing - B Setups (1%) - If anything (up to 3) is missing from above
- i.e. Strong VWAP breaks and inclines, but it’s close to a key level
- A2s painted near VWAPs but the breakthrough isn’t very strong
- Breakthrough a key level and a VWAP but no A2s
Position Sizing - C Setups (0.50%) - If more than 3 is missing
i.e. - VWAP break but not quite enough to take 1R, no A2, and VWAPs are not that inclined.
No Setup - (0.50%) If no grade given, always trade the minimum
